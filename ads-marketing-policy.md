# Ads & Marketing Policy

## Scope
This repository is for planning and operational documentation only.

## Allowed content
- Campaign plans and registry metadata
- Creative briefs and approved copy text
- Landing page copy drafts
- Channel strategy notes
- Tracking and consent policy documents
- Reporting templates and structures

## Prohibited content
- Deployment configuration or runtime changes
- DNS changes
- Cloudflare mutations
- Zaraz enablement
- Pixels or tracking scripts
- Secrets, tokens, passwords, private keys, API credentials
- Customer personally identifiable data

## Data and tool separation
- GitHub: non-secret planning and metadata
- Proton Drive: heavy/raw assets
- Proton Pass: secrets and credentials

## Tracking governance
No pixel or tracking script may be enabled until privacy/consent review is formally approved.
Cloudflare Zaraz remains planning-only until explicit approval.
