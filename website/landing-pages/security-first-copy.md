# Security-First Copy

## Headline
Security-first by system design, not afterthought.

## Subhead
Every provider reference is governed, every claim is controlled, and every tracking capability remains disabled by policy.

## Body
The platform follows a no-secret repository model, text-only provider cards by default, and explicit evidence requirements before any official partnership language.

## Disclaimer
"Technology names are referenced as tools, platforms, providers, or channels used or evaluated by the project. Unless explicitly stated, references do not imply sponsorship, endorsement, certification, or official partnership."
