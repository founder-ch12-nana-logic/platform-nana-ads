# Voice and Tone

## Voice attributes
- Bold
- Premium
- Enterprise-grade
- AI-native
- Future-facing
- Trust-building
- Conversion-focused
- Operator/founder-friendly

## Tone rules
- Lead with outcomes and governance, not hype
- Use direct language with confident specificity
- Prioritize operational clarity and legal-safe claims
- Avoid unverified partnership, endorsement, or certification language

## Style guardrails
- Prefer action verbs: build, govern, scale, secure
- Keep claims measurable and workflow-centered
- Reinforce security and no-tracking posture until activation approval

## Safe disclaimer
"Technology names are referenced as tools, platforms, providers, or channels used or evaluated by the project. Unless explicitly stated, references do not imply sponsorship, endorsement, certification, or official partnership."
