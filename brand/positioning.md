# Platform Nana Positioning

Platform Nana is the **AI-native internal SaaS factory** for operators and founders who need speed, control, and governance in one system.

## Positioning pillars
- AI-native planning, remediation, and operating workflows
- No-code/low-code builder acceleration with controlled release gates
- Security-first workflow from prototype to governed SaaS
- Provider ecosystem orchestration across build, infra, storage, and growth
- Marketplace-ready UI/template velocity for repeatable launches

## Core promise
Build internal tools without chaos, then scale toward a premium provider and template marketplace.

## Market narrative
From prototype to governed SaaS, Platform Nana gives teams one operating system where intelligence, execution, and trust move together.
