# Audience Personas

## Founder-Operator
- Needs speed without operational debt
- Wants one command center for build + growth decisions

## Internal Tools Lead
- Needs low-code velocity and governance controls
- Prioritizes repeatable internal app delivery

## Security-Conscious Product Operator
- Needs private workflows and policy-safe execution
- Rejects uncontrolled tracking and ad-hoc scripts

## Agency Systems Builder
- Needs reusable funnels, templates, and provider ecosystem leverage
- Optimizes for conversion and delivery margin

## Innovation Program Owner
- Needs controlled experimentation with auditable operations
- Wants pipeline from prototype to governed SaaS
